PocketGardener

AI-driven discovery to rapidly explore features, IA, user flows, and early interaction patterns for a new product concept.
Type Passion Project In progress
Stack
Figma
Figma Make
AI-assisted discovery
The concept
A home garden and small-space gardening app that helps users track, notify, diagnose, and document their gardening journey. Built for people who want to grow things but need guidance doing it.
The approach
Used AI-driven discovery to rapidly explore features, information architecture, user flows, and early interaction patterns. An in-depth prototype built in Figma Make walks through onboarding — including an AI-guided flow that adapts to what you're trying to grow.
Still exploring
The visual garden section is still being designed — a spatial planner that maps your growing area with a photo-based grid overlay, daylight heatmap data, and plant placement tools.
