Product & UX Designer

Where the system has an author.

"Good design should always be simple. Even if that takes longer."

Who

I'm a Product and UX Designer who aims to make products that are simple to use and look great. My work is modern and timeless by intention — not by accident. What good design looks like is what I strive to create.

Reliable, confident, and considered. That's how I show up to the work.

Philosophy

"The mistake most designers make is thinking that everything has to be designed perfect."

It doesn't. It just has to be business perfect. When the business need is met, the design is done. Everything else is a bonus.

Design serves the product. The product serves the business. The business serves the user. In that order — but never at the expense of the user.

Why this exists

Design systems are living documents. They grow and contract based on what the work demands.

"Because what's cooler than a product and UX designer having their own design system?"
