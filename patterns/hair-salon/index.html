Patterns / Freelance

Hair Salon Studio Website

The salon had no online presence — no way for new clients to find them, no central place for existing ones to connect. This site changes that.
Role UX / Product Designer Client Freelance In progress
Stack
Figma
Mobile-first design
Problem
A local salon with no digital footprint — invisible to anyone searching online. No website, no booking link, no way to communicate services, pricing, or availability to new clients.
Intervention
Designed a mobile-first website — discoverable, brand-driven, and booking-friendly. Built to work for walk-in clients and first-time visitors searching on their phones.
Design in progress
This project is still being finalized before launch. Design artifacts will be available once the site goes live.
